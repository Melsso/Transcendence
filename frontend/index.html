Instructions And Rules:

- Use the arrow keys to move your paddle up and down.
- ball has to hit the opposing side to you to win the round!
- NO WINDOW RESIZING, that will count as leaving the game.
- You may choose to either play with or without the Power-Ups on the
right of the canvas under the button:"Play Pong".
- First player to reach 7 points wins the game!

POWER UPS:


Power ups are these rectangle blocks that spawn, you may receive the buffs if you manage to hit these blocks
with the ball after it hits your paddle, the power ups are:
~Golden Rectangle: this power up will greately increase your speed, better learn how to play with that buff!
~Red Rectangle: this power up will allow you the chance to have an attack! pressing spacebar will allow you
to shoot a rectangle from the middle of your paddle traveling at high speed towards the other end, if you manage
to hit your opponents Paddle, this will result in him having to play with only half a paddle!

Enjoy playing pong! try all the AI difficulties, I heard the hardest difficulty is unbeatable!
